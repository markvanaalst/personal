Since their are a few hundred Sitecore enthusiasts coming to Amsterdam we thought I would come in handy to share a few links.

If you are coming by plane and you need to travel by public transport, you can plan your journey at this site: [http://9292.nl/en](http://9292.nl/en). It will
plan your route using all public transportation possibilities available. If you only need to have information about the train then you can visit the site of the
Dutch railway company [http://www.ns.nl/en/travellers/home](http://www.ns.nl/en/travellers/home). Also the city of Amsterdam has it own website regarding the
public transport in the region, you can find it at [http://en.gvb.nl/pages/home.aspx](http://en.gvb.nl/pages/home.aspx).

The address of the venue is:

> Hotel Okura Amsterdam
>
> Ferdinand Bolstraat 333
>
> 1072 LH Amsterdam
>
> T: + 31 (0)20 678 71 11
>
> [www.okura.nl](http://www.okura.nl)

- From Station Central: tram 25, stopping-place Cornelis Troostplein. Travelling time ± 20 minutes.
- From Station Zuid: bus 65, stopping-place Scheldestraat. Travelling time ± 8 minutes.
- From Station Amstel: tram 12, bus 15 stopping-place Scheldestraat. Travelling time ± 10 minutes.
- From Station RAI: A 10 minutes walk to the Hotel Okura Amsterdam.

If you would like to know more about Amsterdam and things you can do. You can visit their (Sitecore 6.5!) site
at [http://www.iamsterdam.com](http://www.iamsterdam.com/).
