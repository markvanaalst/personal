Tonight I was catching up with some RSS Feeds and Twitter messages, when I noticed that [SPN](http://spn.sitecore.net) (Sitecore Partner Network) is renewed. My
compliments to the people behind the renewal, it looks so much better and it's easier to find the information you're looking for. Next one is
[SDN](http://sdn.sitecore.net) perhaps? I know I would appreciate it, and I think there are more people who would like it.

When I was crawling through the content of SPN I saw the [roadmap](http://spn.sitecore.net/Products/~/media/Files/Products/Sitecore_Roadmap%2010-09.ashx).
Curious as I am I opened it to see if it was a different version than the the outdated one at the old SPN. I then saw that I it's updated and has some real nice
improvements.

Here are some

- Sitecore Client RSS feeds
- WebDav
- MS Word Fieldtype
- Webforms 2.0
- LINQ data abstraction
- Re-factored publishing engine
- Advanced clustering of any number of Production CMS Servers

Take a look at the [roadmap](http://spn.sitecore.net/Products/~/media/Files/Products/Sitecore_Roadmap%2010-09.ashx), you will find more information there.
