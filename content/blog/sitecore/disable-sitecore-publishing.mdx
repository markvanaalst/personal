To avoid problems during the development of an Sitecore site it could be useful to disable the Sitecore Publishing method.

This can be done by editing the following web.config rule:

<site
  name="website"
  virtualFolder="/"
  physicalFolder="/"
  rootPath="/sitecore/content"
  startItem="/home"
  database="web"
  domain="extranet"
  allowDebug="true"
  cacheHtml="true"
  htmlCacheSize="10MB"
  enablePreview="true"
  enableWebEdit="true"
  enableDebugger="true"
  disableClientData="false"
/>

These are the settings of your current site. The database key contains the database name from where the site needs to get its content. By setting this to the
master database you do not need to publish your content anymore.
